Trajectory Equation

Game

How To Play



y = y0 + (tanθ) * (x - x0) + (ay / (2 * v02)) * (1 + tan2θ) * (x - x0)2


x0: Initial x-position

y0: Initial y-position

v0: Launch velocity

θ: Launch angle

ay: Gravity (-32.2 ft/s or -9.81 m/s)

x: final x-position

y: final y-position


The trajectory equation describes the y-position of an object in projectile motion as a function of its x-position.

However, as long as you have all but one of the variables, you can use the trajectory equation to find the unknown variable.
